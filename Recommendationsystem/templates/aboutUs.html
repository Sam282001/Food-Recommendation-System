About Diet Recommendation


There are many perspectives on nutrition. The most important part of good nutrition is finding an eating pattern that is healthy for you and that fits your needs, circumstances, and lifestyle. To help you navigate nutrition information, here is advice based on both scientific research and personal experiences.Nutrition guidelines generally recommend 130 grams of carbohydrates per day Diet recommends 100-150 grams per day, which is about 25-30% of a person’s daily calories that should come from carbs. This means that if you aim to eat 2,000 calories per day, then about 550 of those calories should come from carbs. We’ve found that it’s easier to stay in range if we limit carbs to 30 grams per meal or snack. You’ll have to experiment and find what’s right for you and your lifestyle.
